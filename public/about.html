About Sightlines

Sightlines is a professional exhibition planning tool for scaled room layouts, wall elevations, artwork placement, and simple 3D preview.

It is built for curators, exhibition planners, preparators, artists, and small gallery or museum teams who need to move between checklist thinking and spatial thinking without giving up privacy, precision, or speed.

Current Product Shape

  • Browser-first and local-first.
  • No account required in v1.
  • No hosted project data or artwork image storage in v1.
  • Project data and uploaded artwork images stay on the user's device.
  • No executable downloads.

Deployment

Sightlines is a static Vite React application deployed on Cloudflare Workers static assets at https://sightlines.art/.
